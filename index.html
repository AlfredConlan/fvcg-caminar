Flathead Valley Campgrounds

Welcome to Flathead Valley Campgrounds

Parting Waters Adventures, Inc., dba Flathead Valley Campgrounds, is the concessionaire of the Flathead National Forest campgrounds located on the Swan Lake, Tally Lake and Hungry Horse – Glacier View Ranger Districts. The 2.4 million acre Flathead National Forest provides habitat to many species of wildlife, including grizzly bear, lynx and bull trout. The forest is bordered by Glacier National Park and Canada and boasts three magnificent wilderness areas; the Bob Marshall Wilderness Area, Great Bear Wilderness Area, and the Mission Mountains Wilderness Area. The forest is the premiere destination for visitors looking to experience wildlife viewing, boating, fishing, hiking, ATV riding, horseback riding and natural landscapes.

Our campgrounds offer:

• Sites located on the banks of Tally Lake, Hungry Horse Reservoir and the North Fork of the Flathead River; others just a cast away from Holland Lake and Swan Lake.

• Many campgrounds are less than 30 minutes to Glacier National Park.

• Clean, quiet, natural wooded settings.

• Accessible facilities, including toilets, picnic tables.

• Designated swimming and day use areas with picnic tables, vault toilets and fire rings.

• Friendly camp hosts, large picnic tables, fire rings, drinking water (hand pumps or pressurized systems), garbage service, and firewood (bundled for sale).

• Boat rentals at Swan Lake, Holland Lake and Tally Lake Campgrounds.

• Group sites available at Tally Lake, Big Creek, Emery Bay, Swan Lake and Holland Lake Campgrounds.

Emery Bay Campground

Emery Bay Campground is located on the east side of Hungry Horse Reservoir approximately 6 miles south of Martin City on the East Side Reservoir Road #38. The campground has 26 standard sites and 2 group sites, each of which can accommodate 75 people, and a concrete boat ramp. The campground is typically open between mid-May and mid-September, weather permitting. Maximum stay is 16 days.

Big Creek Campground

Big Creek Campground is located along the North Fork of the Flathead River, 19 miles north of Columbia Falls, MT. The campground has 22 standard sites (20 single and 2 double), and a group area that has 7 sites and will accommodate around 200 people. Most sites have a river view. There is also a large day use area with a concrete boat launch for river access, parking for approximately 30 vehicles, and a picnic area with 7 picnic tables. The campground is open between mid-May and mid-September, weather permitting. Maximum stay is 16 days.

Doris Creek Campground

Doris Creek Campground and boat launch area is located on the west side of Hungry Horse Reservoir approximately 6 miles south of Hungry Horse, MT. The boat launch area is separate from the campground and has a 70 place parking area with a concrete double-lane boat ramp with a courtesy dock, as well as a day use area with 3 picnic sites, each having a fire ring and picnic table. The campground is about 1 mile from the boat launch area and has 10 standard sites. The campground is typically open between mid-May and mid-September, weather permitting. Maximum stay is 16 days.
